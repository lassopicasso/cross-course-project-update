About us

The idea behind RainyDays Inc. came about after Lars, Bobby and Josephine had been on a though mountain hike. The weather was fluctuating, With snow on the mountain and sleet and rain at lower altitudes. It was tiring to have to bring several types of jackets on the trip. After a good dinner the same night, the friends began to form a vision of a rain jacket that works well in all weather conditions. A year later, in 2017, RainyDays Inc. was established.

We offer rain jackets that are durable, comfortable and suitable for all weather conditions. So keep Pushing the Comfort Zone but do so with comfortable clothing.
